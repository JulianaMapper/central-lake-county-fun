About & Terms of Use

It is not a for-profit endeavor, has no affiliation with the libraries, park districts, forest preserves, or municipalities listed, and earns no money from the listings.

Use at your own risk. Events, times, locations, prices, registration requirements, and program availability change frequently. Listings are aggregated from public calendars and may contain errors, omissions, or out-of-date information. Always confirm details directly with the organizer before traveling to or relying on any event. Accessibility. Accessibility is an ongoing effort — if you encounter a barrier, please email so it can be fixed.

Privacy. This site sets no tracking cookies, runs no analytics, and collects no personal information. The "bucket list" check-offs are stored only in your own browser's local storage.
